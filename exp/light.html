LIGHT MAGIC




Light Magic is the oldest form of Magic known to the Anvar and is said to have been learnt in a feverish wish to be more like Solar. It is the reason for the schism that seperated the Western Anvar from the rest of their race on the Eastern landmasses. Light Magic typically appears as blindig lights, or energy that burns like the sun.
Forbidden Magic
Like all of the Recognized Forms of Magic, Light Magic is a method for incredibly powerful spells to be cast, and thus, has it's own Forbidden Magic. Locked away by the Magus Council, these Spells are too powerful for any but the most stalwart of heroes to weild.
  • Force of Light
    Utilizing this Forbidden Spell, the Caster launches a white orb of blinding light. The inside of such an orb is incredibly hot; the temperature approaching fusion levels, and the light emitted from it truely blinding.. Contact with such an orb will literally burn through the target, as the unbelievably violent energies act almost like a grouping of drill. After such an impact occures, the orb continues moving; allowing it to strike multiple targets. Due to the nature of this spell, it's course is quite erratic.
  • Solar's Gaze
    Unleashing the wrath of Solar upon the ground, the Light Mage causes a powerful beam of energy to decend from the heavens to strike a particular spot. The beam happens to be half a kilometer across.


Magic Political Information

Light Magic is the magic from which both Holy and Illusion were developed. As such it maintains good connections with both these branches of magic, though only Holy Magic can truely be called an ally. With it's roots in the power fo Solar, and embued as it is with the might of Solgaran, Light Magic is both powerful and uncontrolled. This makes it a prime antithesis to both the Order and the Guardians, a situation only increased by the blossoming war between West and East.


The Symbol of the Light Sect is the Blazing Sun.

The most powerful Light Magic user is the Anvar Paladin, Ib.

General Allies: Holy
General Hostilities: Corrupt, Dark, Order, Guardian
Main Goals of Light Magic: Defend the Western Anvar
