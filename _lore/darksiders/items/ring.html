Darksiders Ring

A ring worn by most Darksiders which is used as a weapon of last resort, concealed on their person with powerful charms; the ring uses the same technology as a Blitz ball to provide a number of emergency casts of a single spell. It can be recharged quickly with a special charging device present in all Darksider bases, otherwise will take around a day to self charge back to full power.
